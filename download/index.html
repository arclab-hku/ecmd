Data Format


We provide all data sequences in rosbag files (various sensors including event streams, infrared images, standard images, LiDAR point clouds, IMU) and text files (Ground truth).

  • /DAVIS346_left/events (dv_ros_msgs/EventArray): Events from the left DAVIS346.
  • /DAVIS346_left/image (sensor_msgs/Image): Color images from the left DAVIS346.
  • /DAVIS346_left/imu (sensor_msgs/Imu): 6-axis IMU from the left DAVIS346.
  • /DAVIS346_right/events (dv_ros_msgs/EventArray): Events from the right DAVIS346.
  • /DAVIS346_right/image (sensor_msgs/Image): Color images from the right DAVIS346.
  • /DAVIS346_right/imu (sensor_msgs/Imu): 6-axis IMU from the right DAVIS346.
  • /DVXplorer_left/events (dv_ros_msgs/EventArray): Events from the left DVXplorer.
  • /DVXplorer_left/imu (sensor_msgs/Imu): 6-axis IMU from the left DVXplorer.
  • /DVXplorer_right/events (dv_ros_msgs/EventArray): Events from the right DVXplorer.
  • /DVXplorer_right/imu (sensor_msgs/Imu): 6-axis IMU from the right DVXplorer.
  • /camera_left/image_color/compressed (sensor_msgs/CompressedImage): Color images from the left standard cameras.
  • /camera_right/image_color/compressed (sensor_msgs/CompressedImage): Color images from the right standard cameras.
  • /hikrobot_infrared_camera/rgb (sensor_msgs/Image): Infrared images from the hikrobot infrared camera.
  • /left/lslidar_point_cloud (sensor_msgs/PointCloud2): Point clouds from the left slanted LiDAR.
  • /right/velodyne_points (sensor_msgs/PointCloud2): Point clouds from the right slanted LiDAR.
  • /velodyne_points (sensor_msgs/PointCloud2): Point clouds from the top-installed LiDAR.
  • /imu/data (sensor_msgs/Imu): 9-axis IMU from the Xsens Mti-30.
  • gt.txt (TUM format): The ground truth signals from centimeter-level GNSS-RTK/INS system.

The following content is divided into two sections:

1. Visualization.

2. Summary Table.
